Inspiring the Next Generation: Techy por el Dia
Last Thursday, we hosted 35 bright young women (ages 14–16) from High School N°16. We gave them a real taste of what it's like to work in a tech team for a day. The goal? To spark their interest in tech careers.
This is our second year joining this initiative by CUTI (the Uruguayan Chamber of IT) for the International Day of Girls in Tech.
